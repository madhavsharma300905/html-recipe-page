Chocolate Chip cookies

Ingredients

  • 2 1/4 cups all purpose flour
  • 1/2 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1 cup unsalted butter, room temperature
  • 1/2 cup granulated sugar
  • 1 cup packed light-brown sugar
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 2 teaspoons pure vanilla extract
  • 2 large eggs
  • 2 cups semisweet and/or milk chocolate chips

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 350°F (180°C). Line baking sheets with parchment paper.
  2. In a small bowl, whisk together the flour and baking soda. Set aside.
  3. In a large bowl, cream the butter and sugars until light and fluffy. Add the salt, vanilla, and eggs, and mix well.
  4. Gradually add the flour mixture to the butter mixture, and stir until combined. Stir in the chocolate chips.
  5. Drop rounded tablespoon-sized balls of dough onto prepared baking sheets, spacing them about 2 inches apart.
  6. Bake for 10-12 minutes, until the cookies are golden brown around the edges but still soft in the center.
  7. Let the cookies cool on the baking sheets for 2 minutes, then transfer to a wire rack to cool completely.

Note:You can store the cookies in an airtight container for up to a week, though they rarely last that long!
